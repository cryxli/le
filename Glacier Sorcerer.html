Skills

Glacier

Glacier is your main Damage Skill. It has insane Damage Multiplier Nodes like Greater Destruction, Morditas' Bane and Endless Frost. This setup focuses on enhancing its third massive explosion as much as possible. Critical Rejuvenation has great synergy with Damage Dealt to Mana Before Health.

  • 1 point into Frost Grip (1/5)
  • 2 points into Resplendent Frost (2/3)
  • 1 point into Critical Rejuvenation (1/1)
  • 1 point into Cold Victory (1/1)
  • 2 points into Breaking Point (2/4)
  • 1 point into Greater Destruction (1/4)
  • 1 point into Morditas' Bane (1/4)
  • 3 points into Greater Destruction (4/4)
  • 2 points into Chilling Force (2/2)
  • 2 points into Greater Chilling (2/2)
  • 1 point into Endless Frost (1/1)
  • 3 points into Morditas' Bane (4/4)
  • 2 points into Breaking Point (4/4)

Ice Barrage

Ice Barrage helps with single target DPS and pierces through enemies thanks to Nailed Shot. Additionally the Frost Armor Node enhances the Damage of Glacier by providing it with an additional Multiplier. Ice Shield completely blocks projectiles coming from the direction it faces for 4 seconds.

  • 2 points into Hail Storm (2/5)
  • 4 points into Cryogenesis (4/4)
  • 1 point into Ice Shield (1/1)
  • 1 point into Shattering Bulwark (1/1)
  • 1 point into Deep Shield (1/1)
  • 2 points into Frost Armor (2/2)
  • 2 points into Biting Cold (2/4)
  • 5 points into Nailed Shot (5/5)
  • 2 points into Sublimation (2/3)

Teleport

Teleport is your Movement Skill. This setup also grants Elemental Resistances, Flat Armour and Stun Immunity for a duration on use. If you want more mobility, don't spec into Mana Tunnel and invest into Elemental Affinity or Mirror Images instead.

  • 3 points into Resonant Plasma (3/5)
  • 5 points into Crystaline Passage (5/5)
  • 1 point into Time Dilation (1/5)
  • 1 point into Mana Tunnel (1/1)
  • 3 points into Tunnel Finder (3/5)
  • 1 point into Rock Phase (1/1)
  • 2 points into Tunnel Finder (5/5)
  • 4 points into Time Dilation (5/5)

Flame Ward

Flame Ward is a powerful Defensive Skill. The Damage Reduction it provides is just insane. You are extremely tanky while it's active but its duration is short! Dual Aegis is a crucial Node, as it provides a second charge for the ability.

  • 2 points into Stalwart Defense (2/5)
  • 1 point into Desperate Defense (1/3)
  • 2 points into Fuel The Flames (2/3)
  • 1 point into Dual Aegis (1/1)
  • 5 points into Barrier (5/5)
  • 3 points into Prismatic Buffer (3/3)
  • 3 points into Dilation (3/3)
  • 2 points into Shrewd Shielding (2/3)
  • 1 point into Mental Aegis (1/5)

Focus

This build utilizes Mana as a defensive layer by stacking Damage Dealt to Mana Before Health and utilizing Glacier's Critical Rejuvenation. Thanks to Null Profusion and Null Infusion, you can use Focus as a panic button that grants a big burst of Mana in situations where you reach negative values.

  • 2 points into Mana Flooded (2/4)
  • 1 point into Desperate Meditation (1/1)
  • 4 points into Revelation (4/4)
  • 1 point into Energy Infusion (1/1)
  • 1 point into Null Profusion (1/1)
  • 3 points into Null Infusion (3/4)
  • 2 points into Mana Flooded (4/4)
  • 4 points into Iron Stance (4/4)
  • 2 points into Chilling Aura (2/5)

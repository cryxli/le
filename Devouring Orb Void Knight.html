Devouring Orb Void Knight

Based on Devouring Orb Void Knight Guide

Gameplay

This Void Knight uses Devouring Orb to summon void orbs that orbit around you. Directly cast Smite while the Devouring Orbs shoot out Abyssal Orb. Whenever Devouring Orb kills an enemy all active Devouring Orbs proc a Void Rift. Use Shield Rush to zoom through maps. Make sure you are familiar with enemy mechanics to know when to use Rebuke to stay alive. Pay attention to their telegraphed attacks!

Skill Rotation

  • Cast Devouring Orb every time it's off cooldown to have as many active as possible.
  • Spam Smite at enemies that withstand the first few hits of Devouring Orb.
  • Use Shield Rush to move across the map.
  • Channel Rebuke when you anticipate taking damage.
  • Use Anomaly to maintain Time Bubble at all times.

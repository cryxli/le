Poison Lich

Based on Lich Leveling Guilde by Terek

This Lich leveling build shines in its offensive capabilities. It melts everything in its way, making it a very solid mastery to play during the campaign.

  • Use Rip Blood as your single target until you unlock Spirit Plague.
  • Use Wandering Spirits on cooldown for clear.
  • Use Spirit Plague for single target and clear.
  • Use Reaper Form and Death Seal on cooldown, without them up the build doesn't work properly.
  • Only use Aura Of Decay once you have spec'ed into it.
  • Before switching to another area you can let Reaper Form fade as it refreshes its cooldown when entering a new area.
  • Always change your staff to the best base possible, until level 38. After this level look only for Spine Rod.
  • Cap your Physical Resistance early to make the campaign look easy.
